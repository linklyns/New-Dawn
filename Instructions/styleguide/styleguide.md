"New Dawn" Website Style Guide: Compassion & Light
This guide establishes the visual identity and user experience parameters for the "New Dawn" website, a digital sanctuary dedicated to the rescue, support, and healing of girls who have experienced abuse. The brand merges the trust and reliability required by modern technology with the profound empathy and safety essential to its mission.

1. Brand Essence
Core Values: Empathy, Safety, Hope, Trust, Strength, Renewal, Professionalism.

Mission: To provide a safe harbor for girls in crisis and to guide them from darkness toward the promise of a "New Dawn."

Target Audience: Girls seeking help, family members, donors, and supporting organizations.

2. Visual System
A. Color Palette: The 'Compassion & Light' Scheme
This scheme uses soft, desaturated tones to create a calming, natural environment, balanced by warm accents that invoke hope.

Primary Background (Light Mode): Soft White (#FFFFFF). Clean, airy space for information.

Secondary Background (Calming Sections): Soft Sky Blue (#A2C9E1). Invokes trust and serenity.

Background Accent (Healing/Growth): Warm Sage Green (#91B191). Symbolizes healing and natural renewal.

Primary Text & Structure (Reliability): Deep Slate Navy (#2D3A4A). The primary contrast color, providing a professional foundation.

Highlight/Hope Color (Energy/Rising Potential): Golden Honey (#FFCC66). Used for key calls-to-action (CTAs), special announcements, and interactive elements.

Warm Compassion/Empathy Color: Coral Pink (#FFE6E1). A friendly, supportive highlight used alongside Golden Honey.

Accessibility & Use
Text on #FFFFFF: Use #2D3A4A.

Text on #A2C9E1: Use #2D3A4A.

Text on #91B191: Use #2D3A4A.

Text on #2D3A4A (Dark Mode/Footer): Use #FFFFFF.

Call-to-Action (CTA) Priority: Use #FFCC66 for primary CTAs (e.g., 'GET HELP NOW') to ensure high-visibility on all backgrounds except warm yellow.

B. Typography: Trustworthy and Approachable
The typography must feel highly professional and trustworthy, yet warm and non-threatening. Avoid rigid, industrial fonts.

Main Headings (H1, H2, H3): A modern, technical, but approachable sans-serif typeface (e.g., Inter or Poppins with slightly rounded terminals), set in Title Case (Hello World, not HELLO WORLD or hello world). This feels gentle yet robust.

Body Text & Sub-sections (P, Li): A clean, exceptionally legible sans-serif for long-form reading (e.g., Montserrat in standard weights).

Text Case: Use "Title Case" for all main headings (e.g., 'Our Story of Hope'). Avoid All Caps, which can feel harsh. Use Sentence case for long descriptions.

Typography in Logo: The main logo wordmark "NEW DAWN" uses the Title Case structure in #2D3A4A. The tagline, "A Path to Healing and Hope," uses Sentence Case in Warm Gray (#606060).

C. Imagery & Graphic Elements
A compassionate, minimalistic, and optimistic aesthetic is essential. Avoid any imagery that depicts distress, isolation, or darkness.

Iconography: The 'Nurtured Bloom'
The central brand symbol is a vector-style icon, contained within the circular, protective form of a light blue hand (#A2C9E1) that foundationally supports all elements. The hand is gently cradling and lifting a young green sprout (#91B191). Behind the sprout is a rising sun, featuring a Warm Yellow (#FFCC66) core and five soft, pastel rays that fan out above (left-to-right as: soft orange, peach, warm yellow, peach, soft orange).

Compositional Graphic Guidelines
Photography: Use soft, natural lighting. Focus on natural, positive imagery of girls in serene, safe environments, showing moments of calm strength, community, or individual growth (e.g., caring for a plant, walking a trail, reading in a safe space). The tone must be clean, light, and hopeful.

Minimalism: The overall site layout must be clean and minimal. Information should be presented with significant whitespace to reduce anxiety. Avoid complex gradients or heavy graphical clutter.

Vector Style: Any supporting icons or graphic elements should be clean, flat, or minimal vector-style, mirroring the simplicity and warmth of the primary "New Dawn" logo.
